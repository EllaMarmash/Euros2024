HOSPITALITY

Tickets

This is such a big tournament in Europe that the process to get tickets is not easy. There are multiple phases for purchasing tickets however, each is a lottery. According to theathletic.com, "the first phase has already concluded with over 1.2 million in ticket sales". The second phase will open on December 2, 2023 to supporters who are from any of the 21 nation participating in the tournament. To participate in the second phase lottery, for authentication reasons, supporters must apply for tickets via the national association of their nation. There are a couple other phases to receiving a ticket for this once in a lifetime event. The last phase involves the UEFA creating a platform for tickets to be resold. This opens in the spring of 2024 to help prevent ticket resale fraud.

*Information from theathletic.com*

 

 

Premium Experiences

The tournament offers the option to not only buy tickets but also to buy premium experiences. Fans can choose from the 10 venues in Germany that will be hosting the 2024 Euros, and purchase one of the many luxurious hospitality experiences. These experiences include complementary parking, access 3 hours before the match and an hour and a half after, walking right into the stadium greeted with a glass of champagne, prime stadium seats, all-inclusive catering and drinks, and private entertainment. There are a couple package options to choose from such as Premium experience for specific venue passes, following the team of your choice to different venues, round of 16, quarterfinal match, or final match.

*Information from uefahospitality.com*
